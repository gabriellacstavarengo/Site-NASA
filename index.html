ECOPods

ECOPods is a project that aims to help and solve multiple problems at once using high technology systems.


With each year that passes we get closer to going to another planet, we also need to take a look back and think about the problems here on earth.

Food and water are two of the main pillars of society, but it is also a growing problem in multiple countries while at the same time being an obstacle in the way of our journey to Mars.

Help with food scarcity and safety around the world while also making a viable system for space travel.
