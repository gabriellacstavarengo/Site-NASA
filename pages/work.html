ECOPods is based on a closed-loop ecological system designed to produce food, oxygen, and clean water using minimal external resources. The pod integrates several existing technologies into one compact, autonomous unit. It will be implemented in diferent areas in both extreme and common environments like: space centers, deserts, urban areas.
With that in mind, its size will be thought of according to the amount of food it needs to produce. This is a representation of what it would look like in a small scale basis.

  • Sensors and monitoring systems
  • The algae bioreactor

    • 3. Algae Bioreactor


    There will be a circular tube running along one side of the pod containing an algae bioreactor The algae consume CO₂ released by:

  • Plant respiration at night
  • breath (if used in habitats)

    • In return, the algae produce:

  • Oxygen
  • Biomass that can be converted into fertilizer or biofuel

    • This helps regulate the internal atmosphere of the pod and supports life-support functions for space or isolated environments.

    4. Environmental Sensors

    Inside the pod, a network of sensors monitors:

  • Temperature
  • Humidity
  • CO₂ and O₂ levels
  • Light intensity
  • pH and nutrient concentration in the water

    • An onboard controller or simple AI system adjusts the internal environment automatically by:

  • Changing LED intensity
  • Adjusting water flow
  • Activating ventilation or cooling
  • Balancing nutrient levels

    • This automation keeps the pod stable without human intervention.
