OUR MAIN GOALS ARE:

  • TECHNOLOGY ADVANCEMENT
  • NEW FOOD PRODUCTION SYSTEM
  • NEW SUSTAINABLE METHODS
  • HELP WITH FOOD SCARCITY
  • IMPROVE SPACE MISSIONS

When talking about the future, ECOPod’s main goals are to solve current challenges and support world advancement. This project has as a baseline the creation of a new sustainable food production system that is climate-resilient, having the ability to work in extreme environments in both Earth and space missions.
