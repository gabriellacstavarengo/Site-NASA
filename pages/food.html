Hydroponic system

Inside the pod, plants grow without soil using hydroponics. Hydroponics reduces water use by up to 90%, needs no fertile soil, and works well in sealed environments. Nutrients are dissolved in water and delivered directly to the roots through:

  • Drip lines
  • Floating rafts
